Quantum Simulator

This project was made during the McGill Physics Hackathon.
Due to the limited time in the event, the GUI wasn't the main priority.
There are two main parts.
First is the GUI with the circuit builder. This part is used to show the dfferent gates and the different states at the different stages of the circuit.
The second part is a proof of concept for the Shor's algorithm. This is only a TUI and has very limited capabilities due to the nature of Shor's algorithm. It can handle at most a product of primes as big as 21. It will then use properties of quantum computers to find the products.
